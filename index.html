Hand Detection - How to Use:

  • Click "Start Detection" to begin
  • Allow camera access when prompted
  • Point your index finger in different directions
  • The detector will show: UP, DOWN, LEFT, or RIGHT
  • Make sure your hand is clearly visible in the camera
  • Keep your index finger extended and pointing in the direction you want to detect

Animal Detection - How to Use:

  • Upload images or take screenshots of animals
  • Type the animal name (e.g., "cat", "dog", "bird")
  • Train a model with your labeled images
  • Test the model to detect animals in real-time
  • Make sure animals are clearly visible in the camera
